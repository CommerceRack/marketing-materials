About Us

Mission Statement:

Continue to develop a truly scalable on-demand e-commerce solution which provides tools and services for our merchants to streamline operations while increasing sales.

CommerceRack is the result of Zoovy, Inc.’s 13 years of experience in the e-commerce merchant services arena.

Brian Horakh founded Zoovy in Feb 2000 as a means for allowing small to mid-size e-commerce businesses a robust, yet affordable eCommerce platform. Zoovy is among the forerunners of marketplace integrated e–commerce solutions such as Amazon.com, Buy.com, eBay, Sears, and many other top comparions shopping services. Zoovy offers complete hosted e–commerce software solutions (SaaS). Zoovy's single source platform brought every aspect of a business together while simplifying processes and allowing product management, multi-channel listings, sales, inventory, shipping, accounting and storefronts to work together while updating in real time.

With over 13 years experience, while supporting 5000 websites, Zoovy, with anyCommerce, continues to be a leader in e-commerce merchant services. Our strategy: The easy things should be easy – the hard things should be possible. Zoovy removes barriers to growth, and allows SMBs to compete at the same level with large corporations.
